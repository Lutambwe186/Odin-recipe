Chinese Tomato and Egg

As a busy student with limited time to cook, Chinese Tomato and Egg became my go to meal for dinner. Served with rice the dish was both delicious and healthy to hit my caloric requiremnts.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 2-3 tsp salt (depends on size of tomatoes)
  • 1-2 tbs sugar (depends on your taste)
  • 1 Scallion
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Chop up Scallion and cut the tomatoes in wedges
  2. Crack eggs into bowl and whisk
  3. Heat up 2 tsb cooking oil on med/high heat
  4. Scramble eggs just until no liquid remain, then take out eggs and set aside
  5. Clean pan and heat another 2 tbs oil
  6. Throw in scallions for 10 seconds, then add the tomatoes
  7. Add salt and sugar then cover pan and let it cook for 2 min
  8. Add in eggs, break eggs apart among tomatoes, make sure all the eggs are covered in the juice
  9. Cook for 30 seconds, garnish with uncooked scallions and serve
